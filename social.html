How to use these assets

Each image renders at 600×314px — exactly half of 1200×628px.
  1. Set browser zoom to 200% (⌘+ on Mac).
  2. Screenshot just the image — Mac: ⌘⇧4, drag. Ignore the label bars.
  3. The screenshot will be exactly 1200×628px — upload directly as your OG image.
